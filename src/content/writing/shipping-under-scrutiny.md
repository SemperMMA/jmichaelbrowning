Most of the work that survives long enough to matter is work that was, at some point, held
up to a light. Not the light of admiration — the light of audit.

## What scrutiny is, exactly

Scrutiny is the moment a stranger reads your work without your context.

> Scrutiny is the moment a stranger reads your work without your context. Build for that stranger.

## Three commitments

### 1. Plain language
If a reader has to ask what a sentence means, you've already lost the argument.

### 2. Decisions on the record
Every decision worth making is worth recording.

### 3. Show your work
The most credible documents are the ones that explain how the conclusion was reached.
